Screen-by-Screen Breakdown

Choose Tour Mode

The initial screen where users select their preferred accessibility mode for the garden tour experience.

Key Features:

  • Simple, clear two-option interface
  • Large, touch-friendly buttons with icons
  • Minimalist design reducing cognitive load
  • High contrast elements for accessibility
Design Philosophy

Clean, intuitive interface that immediately presents the two primary accessibility options without overwhelming the user.

Audio Tour - Location Selection

Two-screen guided audio tour interface: area selection and plant-specific audio content for accessibility-focused navigation.

Screen Features:

  • Screen 1: Choose area (Kibble Palace, Glasshouse, Wild Rose Garden)
  • Screen 2: Select specific plants with audio tour options
  • Voice-over descriptions for each location and plant
  • Play button integration for immediate audio access

Visual & QR Tour

Accessible QR code scanning interface with clear visual feedback, designed specifically for deaf and hard-of-hearing visitors to access location-based content.

QR Scanner Features:

  • Large, clear scanning viewfinder with corner guides
  • High contrast visual indicators for successful scans
  • Intuitive back navigation and clear interface
  • Camera-based QR code detection for instant content access
Accessibility Focus

The QR scanner provides immediate visual feedback and guides users through the scanning process with clear visual cues, making it easy for visitors to access location-specific content.

Audio Tour - Content Player

Guided Audio Tour Player

Immersive audio experience featuring plant-specific content with beautiful botanical imagery, professional narration, and accessible playback controls.

Player Features:

  • High-quality botanical photography with plant identification
  • Professional audio narration with clear pronunciation
  • Large, accessible playback controls (previous, play/pause, next)
  • Progress timeline with current time and total duration
  • Scientific and common plant names displayed
Accessibility Design

Large, contrasting controls make navigation easy for users with visual impairments, while the clear audio and visual presentation supports various accessibility needs.

Visual Plant Information

Comprehensive plant profiles featuring detailed botanical information, conservation status, and educational content designed for visual accessibility.

Information Features:

  • High-quality botanical photography and plant identification
  • Conservation status
  • Scientific and common names
  • Regional distribution maps and ecological information
  • Educational descriptions and botanical characteristics
Educational Design

Clear visual hierarchy, high contrast text, and intuitive information layout make complex botanical data accessible to all visitors, including those with visual or hearing impairments.

Visual Tour - Plant Information

BSL Interpretation Video

Professional British Sign Language interpretation videos providing comprehensive botanical information, featuring qualified BSL interpreters delivering educational content about plant species, conservation status, and botanical characteristics.

BSL Video Features:

  • Professional BSL interpreters with botanical knowledge
  • Clear signing with scientific plant names and fingerspelling
  • High-quality video with optimal lighting for sign visibility
  • Synchronized botanical imagery and plant identification
  • Interactive video controls with pause and replay options
BSL Accessibility Focus

Our BSL interpretation videos ensure deaf visitors receive the same rich botanical education as hearing visitors, with qualified interpreters conveying complex scientific concepts through clear, professional sign language delivery.
